Sail Skill Association

IYT Training Center in Montenegro

Preparation of future skippers through Bareboat Skipper and Yachtmaster programs. We offer you a chance to master another degree of freedom: becoming the skipper of a yacht. Our mission is to give you the skill and ability to create unforgettable time on the water for yourself and the people close to you. Real rest begins when the skipper and crew act with confidence and coordination; that is how good memories and unforgettable yachting are made.

In short

Future skipper training

  • IYT path
  • On-water practice
  • Navigation table
  • Certificate by level
What is included
  • A structured IYT path through Sail Skill Association: Bareboat Skipper, Yachtmaster, VHF and the next level when the participant is ready.
  • Practice on real sailing yachts, motor yachts and catamarans, not only classroom theory.
  • Local Montenegro practice with the rhythm of marina life, weather, berthing and real crew communication.
Navigation table
  • Marine navigation, route planning, charts, lights and signals.
  • COLREGs, meteorology, VHF radio communication and yachting documents.
  • Yacht structure, safety routines, first aid and the logic behind skipper decisions.
On-water practice
  • Handling under engine and under sail, with attention to timing and crew roles.
  • Mooring, anchoring, buoys, marina calls and approach planning.
  • Man overboard manoeuvre, use of equipment and the habits that keep the boat calm.
Who it is for

For beginners, owners, future skippers and crew members who want to understand the boat as a system and learn to act together, not just collect a certificate.

Area

The Montenegro base is Tivat and Porto Montenegro, with nearby Adriatic training waters selected by program, weather and participant level.

Result

After completing the course, the participant receives the certificate corresponding to the level of the completed program. The purpose is not to turn a person into a “student”, but to help a future skipper act calmly and confidently on board: to understand the sea, the boat, the crew, the weather and their responsibility.
