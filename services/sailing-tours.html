What is included
  • A route plan for a week or longer: start and finish ports, passages, anchorages and weather alternatives.
  • The skipper leads the route, helps choose places from the water and keeps the holiday rhythm calm.
  • Grottos, bays, islands, swimming, photos, nights at anchor, marinas and waterfront restaurants where they make sense for the route.
Who it is for

For families, couples, friends and small groups who want a private week-long route, or a shorter trip built with the same calm logic.

Week route ideas

Route ideas are chosen by weather, season, boat, starting point and the desired rhythm of the week.

MontenegroThe Bay of Kotor, Bigova Bay, Luštica, Sveti Stefan and calm coastal stops selected by weather.

CroatiaHvar and the Pakleni Islands, a Krka route through the Šibenik/Skradin area, bays, island stops and waterfront lunches.

ItalyThe Amalfi Coast, Capri and Lipari/the Aeolian Islands, with stops shaped by season, logistics and waterfront restaurants.

Result

Not just an excursion, but a personal sea route: a clear week plan, beautiful stops at the right time and calm decisions on the water.
